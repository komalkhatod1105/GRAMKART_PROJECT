About GramKart

GramKart is a social initiative aimed at empowering rural artisans and farmers by providing them with a platform to sell their products directly to consumers across India.

We eliminate middlemen to ensure fair prices for both producers and customers while maintaining the highest quality standards.

By supporting GramKart, you contribute to the sustainable development of rural communities and help preserve traditional crafts and farming practices.
